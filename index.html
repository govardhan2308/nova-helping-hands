Welcome to Nova Helping Hands

Small Actions, Big Impact

We are a dedicated group of volunteers, social workers, and global contributors working toward eliminating inequality and fostering healthy environments for children and families.

Our Mission

To protect human dignity, provide access to clean water, sound health and quality education, and conserve the environment through active community leadership.

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of their background, possesses the opportunity to lead a healthy, educated, and self-sufficient life.
